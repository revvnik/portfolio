About Me

My name is Nikita. I am an adolescent based in Berlin, Germany. I discovered programming back in 2021 and photography in 2022. Photography quickly became my biggest hobby, which is why you will see it a lot more often than things that have to do with programming. I started with street photography, and while I've always been a big fan of cars and motorcycles, I've started doing specifically automotive photography in 2024. Here, I want to showcase some of my work and maybe even attract new customers! Yes, I also do photoshootings! So if you feel like it: Text me on Instagram! Make sure to follow as well to not miss anything. I promise, after you've seen my photos, you will view your car or bike in a whole new way.
